Frequently asked Questions

The course is design to be completed over a span of 7 months, with a commitment of approximately 5-7 hours per wek. Each week includes video lectures, reading materials, and assignments that help reinforce the concepts learned. While the course is self-paced, we encourage participant to stay on track with the weekly schedule to maximize their learning experience.

By the end of the course, you'll have the skills to build several web applications. Examples include a "personal portfolio website" that showcase your projects and skills, a "dynamic e-commerce platform" with user authentication and payment integration, and a "real-time chat application" using webSockets. Additionally, you will learn to create a "task management tool" similar to Trello, using ReactJS for the front-end, NodeJS and ExpressJS for the backend,and MongoDB as the database.You may also build small applications that integrate with third-party APIs, such as "weather dashboard" that fetches real time data from a weather API. The final Project will be a comprehensive application that incorporates all these skills, serving as capstone to demonstrate your proficiency and creativity, providing you with a portfolio piece to show potential employers or clients

Yes upon successfully completing the course and all of its assessments, you will receive a certificate of completion. This certificate is a great way to showcase your newly acquired skills on your resume, Linkedin profile, or personal website. its is important to note that the certificate is not an official accreditation, but serve as a recognition for your hard work and dedication in completing the course.

This course is designed for beginners, so no prior programming experience is required. However, a basic understanding of how to use a computer and navigate the internet is helpful. we lso recommend having a good grasp of problem-solving skills and logical thinking. All necessary software tools and resources will be provided, and you will be guided step-by-step through the setup and use of each one.

Throughout the course, you will have access to a range of support options. These include a dedicated course forum where you can ask questions and get help from instructors and fellow students, weekly live Q&A sessions with the course instructor, and 24/7 access to all materials. if you encounter any technical issues or have questions about the course content , our support team is available to assist you via email or chat.
